## PART 2 — THE VERNEN COUNTER-ECONOMIC MODEL

### How the Prevention Surface Works

Vernen does not reduce fraud after the fact. It catches the compliance failure before the harm completes.

```
Traditional compliance model:
Violation occurs → Harm absorbed → Complaint filed → Investigation →
Enforcement action → Fine levied → Some recovery → Repeat

Vernen model:
Document/app/communication submitted → Compliance verified in real time →
Violation flagged before completion → Harm prevented → No loss to recover
```

The economic value of prevention is always greater than the economic value of enforcement:
- Enforcement recovers a fraction of losses after legal costs
- Prevention eliminates the loss before it enters the system
