## EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

**What we are:**
Vernen Compliance LLC is a legal compliance technology company based in California. We build autonomous AI compliance agents — called Citizens — that verify whether legal, medical, financial, and governmental interactions comply with the laws that govern them. We are the first company to automate compliance verification at the point of encounter rather than after the harm.

**What the platform does:**
The platform operates at compliance.vernenlegal.com with 745+ compliance rules, 574+ legal standards, and 7 operational AI compliance agents live in production today. Each agent is trained on federal enforcement data and governing statutes for a specific professional domain — civil rights law, family law, consumer protection, criminal law, victim compensation, and more. When a citizen submits a document, the platform instantly identifies every applicable compliance framework, audits the document against it, and generates a finding with the specific statute violated and the severity.

**The market gap we close:**
- The law says apps must disclose what data they collect. Our 560-app scan found 4.08 billion cumulative installs where compliance is unverified.
- The law says court interpreters must be certified and accurate. No real-time verification layer exists.
- The law says cellular networks must be what they claim to be. SIM swap fraud costs Americans $68M+ per year — a documented floor.
- The law says contracts must disclose certain terms. Most people have no way to verify this at the moment of signing.

Vernen is the verification layer that has never existed.

**What we've built without outside capital:**
- Live platform with Stripe payments, production database, API endpoints
- 7 operational Citizens serving civil rights, family law, consumer protection, criminal law, real estate, and victim compensation
- 560-app Google Play compliance scan already operational
- Smart glasses application (Android XR) for real-time visual compliance verification
- Complete five-fork architecture: CITIZEN · Visual · Audio · Communications · Software
- Vernen Compliance LLC filed with the California Secretary of State, April 10, 2026

**Who built it:**
