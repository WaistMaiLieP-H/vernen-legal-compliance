# EXHIBIT A — STATEMENT OF WORK

## DEEP FORENSIC SCAN

**Engagement Fee:** Seven Thousand Five Hundred Dollars ($7,500.00)
**Initial Payment:** $3,750.00 (50%) due on execution
**Final Payment:** $3,750.00 (50%) due on delivery of Forensic Scan Report
**Delivery Window:** 14 calendar days from receipt of Initial Payment and complete document set

### Scope of Document Set

The Deep Forensic Scan covers a single document set consisting of:

- Up to **fifty (50) discrete documents** (contracts, leases, settlement statements, agency findings, court filings, correspondence, or related instruments); and
- Up to **five hundred (500) pages** in aggregate.

Documents exceeding these limits require a written change order under §10 of the Agreement, billed at $0.40 per additional page or $200 per additional document, whichever is greater.

### Analysis Performed

For each document and across the document set as a whole, Vernen will run the **Layer 7 Structural Forensic Analysis** stack:

1. **Temporal Paradox Filter (Layer 7a).** Detects effects that predate causes — entity formation after execution, citation of authorities post-dating the document, regulatory amendments cited before their effective date, insurance certificates referencing dates before the issuing entity existed, and similar structural impossibilities.

2. **Circular Entity & Liability Mapping (Layer 7b).** Builds a relational graph across all named entities. Detects shared addresses, shared registered agents, EIN-prefix clustering, formation-week clustering, and accountability vacuums where revenue, liability, equipment ownership, and indemnity are split across entities sharing common attributes.

3. **De Facto Reclassification Engine (Layer 7c).** Scores worker-relationship contracts against AB5 ABC, IRS Rev. Rul. 87-41, and DOL economic-reality tests. Surfaces control language across seven categories (work assignment, time, location, equipment, method, exclusivity, discipline) and computes a jurisdictional verdict.

4. **Unconscionability / Predatory-Math Trigger (Layer 7d).** Performs an actuarial analysis of any financial terms — recomputes effective APR against disclosed APR (TILA / Reg Z framework), identifies disguised security interests under UCC §1-203, surfaces confession-of-judgment, jury and class waivers, no-cure clauses, one-sided fee shifting, choice-of-law evasion, and forum-selection oppression.

### Deliverables

A **written Forensic Scan Report** containing, for each document:

- Document identifier and page reference
- Each finding with severity, evidence excerpt, and statutory or regulatory citation
- The Vernen verdict at each layer
- A document-set summary across all four layers
- A unified risk score and recommendation

Plus:

- A **verification hash anchor** allowing any third party to confirm the report has not been altered after delivery (see §4(b) of the Agreement)
- **One (1) sixty-minute clarification call** following delivery

### Out of Scope

The following are explicitly **not** part of the Deep Forensic Scan:

- Legal advice, legal strategy, or representations about the legal effect of any finding (see §6 of the Agreement)
- Court filings, pleadings, or correspondence with opposing parties
- Negotiation with third parties on Client's behalf
- Witness interviews, depositions, or testimony
- Forensic accounting beyond the document text itself
- Document recovery, scanning, or OCR (documents must be delivered in machine-readable form)
- Translation of non-English documents
- Expert witness services or court testimony

Any of the above may be added under a separate written engagement.

### Acceptance

Client's payment of the Final Payment under §3.2 of the Agreement constitutes acceptance of the Forensic Scan Report.
