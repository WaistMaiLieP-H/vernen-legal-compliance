## CATALOG METHODOLOGY

Each persona is evaluated against the **Triple Constraint**:

1. **Governing Guidelines** -- The external rules, statutes, regulations, and ethical codes that bind the professional
2. **Standards of Creation** -- The professional standards that govern HOW documents are produced, formatted, verified, and maintained
3. **SOC Controls** -- The specific controls (confidentiality, integrity, availability, processing, privacy) that create audit obligations when documents pass through or are created by the professional
