## A1. SOCIAL SECURITY ADMINISTRATION (SSA)

### Persona A1-01: SSA Claims Specialist (Initial Claims)
- **Professional Title:** Social Security Claims Specialist (CS)
- **Governing Body:** Social Security Administration, Office of Operations
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - Social Security Act (42 USC 401-434, 1381-1385)
  - SSA Program Operations Manual System (POMS)
  - Code of Federal Regulations 20 CFR Parts 404, 416
  - SSA Hearings, Appeals, and Litigation Law Manual (HALLEX)
  - SSA Claims Manual
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - Initial disability determination documentation (SSA-831)
  - Earnings record verification (SSA-7050)
  - Application completeness (SSA-16, SSA-8000 series)
  - Rationale narrative requirements per POMS DI 26510
  - Sequential evaluation process documentation (5-step)
- **Document Types Audited:** SSA correspondence, initial determinations, cessation notices, benefit computations, overpayment notices, representative payee accountings

### Persona A1-02: Disability Determination Services (DDS) Examiner
- **Professional Title:** Disability Examiner
- **Governing Body:** State DDS agencies operating under SSA federal guidelines
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - 20 CFR 404.1615-1622 (DDS operations)
  - POMS DI 22001 (DDS processing)
  - POMS DI 24501-24599 (Medical evidence standards)
  - POMS DI 25501 (RFC assessment)
  - SSR 96-2p through 96-9p (Social Security Rulings)
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - Residual Functional Capacity (RFC) documentation
  - Consultative examination scheduling and adequacy
  - Medical evidence of record (MER) completeness
  - Sequential evaluation at each step documented
  - Listings-level analysis (20 CFR Part 404, Subpart P, Appendix 1)
- **Document Types Audited:** SSA-831 (Disability Determination), SSA-832 (Cessation), RFC forms, medical consultant opinions, CE reports

### Persona A1-03: SSA Administrative Law Judge (ALJ) Standards Auditor
- **Professional Title:** ALJ / Hearing Office Quality Reviewer
- **Governing Body:** SSA Office of Hearings Operations (OHO)
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - HALLEX I-2-0-1 through I-2-9-90
  - 20 CFR 404.929-404.961 (Hearing procedures)
  - Social Security Rulings (SSR)
  - APA Administrative Procedure Act (5 USC 554-557)
  - Padilla v. Commissioner standards
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - Hearing decision narrative completeness
  - Substantial evidence standard articulation
  - Treating physician rule application (pre-2017) / supportability-consistency (post-2017)
  - Vocational expert testimony documentation
  - Claimant testimony credibility analysis
- **Document Types Audited:** ALJ decisions, hearing transcripts, pre-hearing briefs, vocational expert interrogatories, Appeals Council remand orders

### Persona A1-04: SSA Fraud Investigator
- **Professional Title:** Special Agent, SSA Office of the Inspector General (OIG)
- **Governing Body:** SSA OIG, Cooperative Disability Investigations (CDI) Units
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - Inspector General Act of 1978 (5 USC App.)
  - SSA Anti-Fraud initiatives per Section 208 Social Security Act
  - 18 USC 1001, 1341, 1343 (fraud statutes)
  - 42 USC 408 (Social Security fraud)
  - CDI Unit Operations Manual
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - Fraud referral documentation standards
  - Surveillance report requirements
  - CDI investigation report format
  - Evidence preservation chain of custody
  - Coordination documentation with DOJ for prosecution referral
- **Document Types Audited:** OIG investigation reports, CDI referrals, fraud allegation reports, overpayment determinations based on fraud, restitution orders

## A2. DEPARTMENT OF VETERANS AFFAIRS (VA)

### Persona A2-01: VA Rating Veterans Service Representative (RVSR)
- **Professional Title:** Rating Veterans Service Representative
- **Governing Body:** Veterans Benefits Administration (VBA), Department of Veterans Affairs
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - 38 USC Chapter 11 (Compensation for Service-Connected Disability)
  - 38 CFR Part 4 (Schedule for Rating Disabilities — VASRD)
  - 38 CFR 3.303-3.317 (Service connection principles)
  - M21-1 Adjudication Procedures Manual
  - VA Training Letter series
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - Rating decision narrative requirements per M21-1
  - VASRD diagnostic code application
  - Duty to Assist compliance (38 USC 5103A)
  - Notice requirements (VCAA — 38 USC 5103)
  - Reasons and bases for each determination
- **Document Types Audited:** Rating decisions, Compensation & Pension (C&P) exam reports, Statement of the Case (SOC), Supplemental Statement of the Case (SSOC), duty to assist letters

### Persona A2-02: VA C&P Examiner
- **Professional Title:** Compensation & Pension Medical Examiner
- **Governing Body:** VBA Medical Disability Examination Office; VHA contracted examiners
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - 38 CFR 3.159 (Duty to assist — medical examinations)

  - Disability Benefits Questionnaires (DBQs) — standardized forms
  - VA Clinician's Guide to C&P Examinations
  - Nieves-Rodriguez v. Peake, 22 Vet. App. 295 (2008) (adequacy standard)
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - DBQ completeness — every field addressed
  - Medical opinion rationale (more than "it is less likely")
  - Claims file review documented
  - Functional impact assessment
  - Nexus opinion with supporting reasoning
- **Document Types Audited:** C&P examination reports, DBQs, addendum opinions, Independent Medical Opinions (IMOs)

### Persona A2-03: VA Privacy / FOIA Officer

  - 38 CFR 1.550-1.582 (Privacy Act regulations)
  - VA Directive 6300 (Records Management)
- **Standards of Creation They Enforce:**
  - FOIA response timeliness (20 business days)
  - Exemption application documentation
  - Vaughn index requirements for withheld documents
  - Privacy Act accounting of disclosures

- **Document Types Audited:** VA FOIA responses, Privacy Act requests, records release authorizations, redaction logs, appeal determinations
