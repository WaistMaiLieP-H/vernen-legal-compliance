# TRIPLE CONSTRAINT FRAMEWORK

For each persona, the Triple Constraint requires:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** -- The statute, regulation, or professional standard that creates the document obligation
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** -- The form, format, content, retention, and procedural requirements the document must satisfy
3. **SOC CONTROLS** -- The segregation of duties, access controls, review/approval workflows, and audit trail requirements that ensure document integrity

A persona is cataloged ONLY when all three constraints produce auditable document obligations.
