## THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT

Every persona in this catalog is bound by three interlocking obligation layers:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** -- The statute, regulation, or agency rule that creates the legal obligation
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** -- The professional, industry, or technical standard that dictates HOW the document must be created, formatted, signed, and preserved
3. **SOC CONTROLS** -- The audit controls (confidentiality, integrity, availability, processing integrity, privacy) that govern how the document is stored, transmitted, verified, and retained

A persona exists wherever all three constraints intersect on a document type. No document escapes the triple constraint.
