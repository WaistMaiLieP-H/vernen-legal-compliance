# SECTION A: TELECOMMUNICATIONS (INDUSTRY)

## A1 — Spectrum & Licensing Subspecialty

### PERSONA A1-01: FCC Licensing Specialist
- **Professional Title:** FCC Licensing Specialist / Spectrum Coordinator
- **Governing Body:** Federal Communications Commission (FCC)
- **Governing Guidelines:**
  - 47 U.S.C. § 301 (License requirement for radio transmission)
  - 47 U.S.C. § 307 (Granting of licenses)
  - 47 U.S.C. § 308 (Requirements for applications)
  - 47 U.S.C. § 309 (Application processing)
  - 47 CFR Part 1, Subpart F (Commission actions on applications)
  - 47 CFR Part 2 (Frequency allocations and radio treaty matters)
  - 47 CFR Part 22 (Public mobile services)
  - 47 CFR Part 24 (Personal communications services)
  - 47 CFR Part 27 (Miscellaneous wireless communications services)
  - 47 CFR Part 90 (Private land mobile radio services)
  - 47 CFR Part 101 (Fixed microwave services)
- **Standards of Creation:**
  - FCC Form 601 (Application for Wireless Telecommunications Bureau Radio Service Authorization)
  - FCC Form 602 (Ownership Disclosure Information)
  - FCC Form 603 (Assignment/Transfer of Control)
  - FCC ULS (Universal Licensing System) electronic filing standards
  - FCC auction qualification and bidding procedures
- **SOC Controls:** SOC 2 Type II — Security, Availability, Confidentiality; access controls on spectrum databases; audit trail for license modifications
- **Document Types Audited:**
  - FCC license applications and renewals
  - Spectrum allocation documents
  - FCC auction participation records
  - License assignment/transfer documents
  - Spectrum lease agreements
  - Construction permit applications

  - 47 CFR Part 15 (Radio frequency devices)
  - 47 CFR Part 18 (Industrial, scientific, and medical equipment)
  - 47 CFR Part 25 (Satellite communications)
  - NTIA Manual of Regulations and Procedures for Federal Radio Frequency Management (Redbook)
  - ITU Radio Regulations (international spectrum coordination)
- **Standards of Creation:**
  - IEEE 802.11 (Wi-Fi spectrum use)
  - IEEE 802.16 (WiMAX)
  - 3GPP TS 36/38 series (LTE/5G NR spectrum)
  - ANSI/TIA-603 (Land mobile radio)

- **Document Types Audited:**
