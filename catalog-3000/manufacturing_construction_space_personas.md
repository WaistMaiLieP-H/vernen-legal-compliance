# THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT

For each persona, three constraints govern document audit obligations:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** — The regulatory or professional authority that mandates the persona's existence and defines their scope of practice
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** — The specific standards, codes, and frameworks that define how documents under their authority must be created, formatted, maintained, and retained
3. **SOC CONTROLS** — The System and Organization Controls (security, availability, processing integrity, confidentiality, privacy) that apply to documents they audit

## A1. Quality Management (ISO 9001 / General)

### A1-001: Quality Management Representative (QMR)
- **Professional Title:** Quality Management Representative
- **Governing Body:** International Organization for Standardization (ISO); American Society for Quality (ASQ)
- **Governing Guidelines:**
  - ISO 9001:2015, Clause 5.3 (Organizational roles, responsibilities, and authorities)

- **SOC Controls:**

- **Document Types Audited:** ISO 9001 quality management documents, non-conformance reports, corrective action reports (CAPA), management review records, internal audit reports, quality policy documents, quality objectives tracking
