## METHODOLOGY

Every professional title listed here was identified because governing guidelines, standards of creation, or SOC controls **name that profession** as having document audit obligations. If any one of the three constraints requires the persona, it is listed. No exceptions.

**Triple Constraint Applied:**
1. **Governing Guidelines** — Statutes/regulations that create the document obligation
2. **Standards of Creation** — Professional standards defining how documents must be created
3. **SOC Controls** — Security/integrity frameworks verifying document authenticity
