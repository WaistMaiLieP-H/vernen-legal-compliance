# SECTION A: DEFENSE CONTRACTING / MILITARY

## A1. Contract Formation & Administration

### A1-01. Government Contracts Attorney
- **Governing Body:** State Bar (jurisdiction-specific); ABA Section of Public Contract Law
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - Federal Acquisition Regulation (FAR), 48 CFR Ch. 1
  - Defense Federal Acquisition Regulation Supplement (DFARS), 48 CFR Ch. 2
  - ABA Model Rules of Professional Conduct, Rules 1.1 (Competence), 1.6 (Confidentiality), 1.13 (Organization as Client)
  - Competition in Contracting Act (CICA), 41 U.S.C. 3301-3308
  - Truth in Negotiations Act (TINA), 10 U.S.C. 3701-3708
- **Standards of Creation:** J.D. from ABA-accredited law school; bar admission; specialized knowledge of FAR/DFARS procurement law
- **SOC Controls:** Access control to classified contract data (SOC 2 CC6.1); confidentiality commitments (CC2.3); document retention per FAR 4.703 (6-year retention)
- **Document Types Audited:** Government contract awards (FAR/DFAR), DFARS compliance documents, organizational conflict of interest documents, small business subcontracting plans, foreign military sales documents, cost accounting standards (CAS) reports

### A1-02. Contracting Officer (CO) / Contracting Officer's Representative (COR)
- **Governing Body:** Defense Acquisition University (DAU); Federal Acquisition Institute (FAI)
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - FAR Part 1.602 (Authority of Contracting Officers)
  - DFARS 201.602 (Contracting Officer responsibilities)
  - DoD Instruction 5000.72 (DoD Standard for Contracting Officer's Representative Certification)
  - FAR Part 42 (Contract Administration and Audit Services)
  - OMB Circular A-123 (Management's Responsibility for Enterprise Risk Management and Internal Control)
- **Standards of Creation:** FAC-C certification (Federal Acquisition Certification in Contracting); DAU courses (CON 090, CON 100, CON 200, CON 360); minimum education and experience per FAR 1.603
- **SOC Controls:** Segregation of duties (CC5.1); authorization controls for contract modifications (CC6.1); audit trail for contract actions (CC7.2)
- **Document Types Audited:** Government contract awards (FAR/DFAR), contractor performance assessment reports (CPARS), DCMA inspection documents, small business subcontracting plans, organizational conflict of interest documents

### A1-03. Government Contracts Specialist / Procurement Analyst
- **Governing Body:** Federal Acquisition Institute (FAI); National Contract Management Association (NCMA)
- **Key Governing Guidelines:**
  - FAR Parts 5-6 (Publicizing/Competition Requirements)
  - FAR Part 15 (Contracting by Negotiation)
  - FAR Part 19 (Small Business Programs)
  - Small Business Act, 15 U.S.C. 631 et seq.
  - NCMA Contract Management Body of Knowledge (CMBOK)
- **Standards of Creation:** FAC-C certification or NCMA Certified Federal Contracts Manager (CFCM); bachelor's degree; 24 semester hours in business disciplines per FAR 1.603-1
- **SOC Controls:** Source selection documentation integrity (CC7.1); bid evaluation audit trails (CC7.2); small business compliance monitoring (CC4.1)
- **Document Types Audited:** Government contract awards (FAR/DFAR), small business subcontracting plans, DFARS compliance documents, organizational conflict of interest documents
