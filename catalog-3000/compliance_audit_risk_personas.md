## THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT

Every persona below is evaluated against three axes:

1. **Governing Guidelines** — What authority says this professional must exist and what they must do
2. **Standards of Creation** — What certifications, qualifications, or standards govern how this professional is made
3. **SOC Controls / Document Audit Obligations** — What documents this professional must create, review, or audit
