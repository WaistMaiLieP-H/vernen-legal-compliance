## THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT

Every persona identified below is subjected to three simultaneous obligations:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** — The law, regulation, or professional code that creates the duty
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** — The format, content, and procedural requirements for documents produced under the duty
3. **SOC CONTROLS** — The security, integrity, access, and audit trail requirements that govern document handling

A persona appears in this catalog ONLY if all three constraints converge to create a document audit obligation.
