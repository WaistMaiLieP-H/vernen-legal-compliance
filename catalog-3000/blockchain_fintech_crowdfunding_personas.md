# THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT

Every persona in this catalog is defined by the intersection of three mandatory dimensions:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** -- The law, regulation, or professional standard that grants authority and imposes obligations on the professional. If the guideline does not apply, the persona has no jurisdiction.
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** -- The formal requirements for how documents must be created, formatted, executed, retained, and transmitted. If the document does not meet the standard, it is defective on its face.
3. **SOC CONTROLS** -- The operational controls (access, integrity, availability, confidentiality, processing) that must be in place when the document is created, stored, transmitted, or audited. If the control is absent, the document chain is compromised.

A document audit obligation exists ONLY where all three constraints converge. A persona is cataloged ONLY where it sits at that intersection.
