## THE TRIPLE CONSTRAINT (Applied to Every Persona)

Each persona is evaluated against three mandatory axes:

1. **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** -- The statutes, regulations, and professional standards that define the persona's legal authority and obligations.
2. **STANDARDS OF CREATION** -- The education, licensure, certification, or appointment requirements that authorize the persona to exist and practice.
3. **SOC CONTROLS** -- The segregation of controls, audit trails, record-keeping mandates, and compliance checkpoints the persona must enforce or verify.
