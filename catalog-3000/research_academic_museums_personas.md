# TRIPLE CONSTRAINT FRAMEWORK

Every persona is defined by three interlocking audit obligations:

| Constraint | Question | Failure Mode |
|---|---|---|
| **GOVERNING GUIDELINES** | What law, regulation, or professional standard controls this document? | Regulatory non-compliance; loss of funding, accreditation, or licensure |

| **SOC CONTROLS** | What security, integrity, access, and retention controls must be applied throughout the document lifecycle? | Data breach; spoliation; chain-of-custody failure; fraud |

### PERSONA A1-01: Research Grants Administrator

**Professional Title:** Research Grants Administrator / Pre-Award Specialist
**Governing Body:** Office of Management and Budget (OMB); NIH Office of Extramural Research; NSF Office of Budget, Finance & Award Management; Council on Governmental Relations (COGR); National Council of University Research Administrators (NCURA)

**Governing Guidelines:**
- 2 CFR Part 200 (Uniform Administrative Requirements, Cost Principles, and Audit Requirements for Federal Awards) -- "Uniform Guidance"
- 2 CFR Part 200, Subpart E (Cost Principles), Subpart F (Audit Requirements)
- NIH Grants Policy Statement (NIH GPS), current edition
- NSF Proposal & Award Policies & Procedures Guide (PAPPG), NSF 25-1
- 48 CFR Part 31 (FAR Cost Principles for contracts)
- OMB Circular A-21 (superseded by 2 CFR 200 but still referenced in legacy awards)
- Single Audit Act Amendments of 1996 (31 U.S.C. 7501-7507)

**Standards of Creation:**
- SF-424 (R&R) Application Guide for NIH
- NSF FastLane / Research.gov submission format requirements
- Grants.gov system-to-system (S2S) XML schema requirements
- Budget justification narrative format per agency-specific instructions
- Facilities & Administrative (F&A) rate agreement documentation (DHHS-negotiated)
- Cost-sharing commitment documentation per 2 CFR 200.306

**SOC Controls:**
- SOC-ACCESS: Role-based access to grants management systems (eRA Commons, Research.gov)
- SOC-INTEGRITY: Budget calculations traceable to institutional rate agreements
- SOC-RETENTION: 3 years from submission of final expenditure report per 2 CFR 200.334
- SOC-AUDIT: Subject to A-133/Single Audit if federal expenditures exceed $750,000
- SOC-SEGREGATION: Separation of pre-award and post-award functions

**Document Types Audited:**
Research grant applications (NIH, NSF, DOE, DOD), grant progress reports, sponsored research agreements, graduate student funding documents, research data management plans

Export control in research (deemed exports), material transfer agreements, sponsored research agreements, research compliance training records

### PERSONA A2-01: IRB Chair / Member

**Professional Title:** Institutional Review Board Chair / IRB Member
**Governing Body:** Office for Human Research Protections (OHRP), HHS; FDA (for FDA-regulated research); institutional Human Research Protection Program (HRPP)

**Governing Guidelines:**
- Common Rule (45 CFR Part 46, Subparts A-E) -- Federal Policy for the Protection of Human Subjects
- 45 CFR 46 Subpart B (Pregnant Women, Fetuses, Neonates)
- 45 CFR 46 Subpart C (Prisoners)
- 45 CFR 46 Subpart D (Children)
- 21 CFR Parts 50 (Informed Consent) and 56 (IRBs) for FDA-regulated research
- ICH E6(R2) Good Clinical Practice (GCP) Guidelines
- Belmont Report (ethical principles)
- AAHRPP Standards (accreditation body)

**Standards of Creation:**
- IRB application forms (initial review, continuing review, amendments, reportable events)
- Protocol review documentation with risk-benefit assessment
- Quorum and voting records per 45 CFR 46.108
- Conflict of interest documentation for IRB members per protocol
- Minutes documenting discussion of controverted issues per 45 CFR 46.115(a)(2)
- Determination letters (approval, modifications required, deferral, disapproval)

**SOC Controls:**
- SOC-INTEGRITY: Documented determination for each protocol action with regulatory criteria
- SOC-ACCESS: IRB records accessible to OHRP/FDA upon request per 45 CFR 46.115(b)
- SOC-RETENTION: 3 years after completion of research per 45 CFR 46.115(b)
- SOC-INDEPENDENCE: IRB membership composition requirements (non-scientist, unaffiliated member) per 45 CFR 46.107
- SOC-AUDIT: For-cause and not-for-cause OHRP compliance evaluations

**Document Types Audited:**
IRB applications/approvals, human subjects research consent forms, research protocol documents, conflict of interest disclosure documents, certificate of confidentiality documents, federalwide assurance (FWA) documents

Human subjects research consent forms, certificate of confidentiality documents, IRB applications/approvals, research protocol documents
