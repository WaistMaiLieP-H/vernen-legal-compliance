## A.1 — RATE REGULATION & TARIFF SUBSPECIALTY

### Persona A.1.1: Utility Rate Case Analyst

- **Governing Guidelines**:

  - Cal. Pub. Util. Code § 451 (just and reasonable rates)
  - Cal. Pub. Util. Code § 728 (commission authority to fix rates)
  - CPUC General Order 96-B (Industry Rules — rate filing requirements)
  - 16 CFR Part 18 (FTC Guides for utility advertising)
  - FERC 18 CFR Part 35 (filing of rate schedules — wholesale)
  - CPUC Standard Practice U-27-W/E/G (rate schedule format)
- **Standards of Creation**: Rate case filings must include testimony, workpapers, cost-of-service studies, revenue requirement calculations, load forecasts, and rate design proposals per CPUC Rules of Practice and Procedure. All financial data must comply with FERC Uniform System of Accounts (18 CFR Part 101 electric, Part 201 gas). Documents must be served on all parties of record and filed electronically via CPUC Docket Office.
- **SOC Controls**: SOC 2 Type II (data integrity of financial projections); internal audit of revenue requirement models; segregation of duties between rate design and cost allocation; version control on all testimony drafts; retention per CPUC GO 107 (minimum 3 years after rate case decision).
- **Document Types Audited**: General Rate Case (GRC) applications, Cost of Capital (CoC) applications, rate design proposals, testimony and workpapers, advice letters (Tier 1/2/3), tariff schedule sheets, preliminary/rebuttal/supplemental testimony, settlement agreements, rate comparison exhibits
